Payment Gateway

Learn how payment gateways securely collect, encrypt and transmit payment information between customers, merchants and payment systems.

What is a Payment Gateway?

A Payment Gateway is a technology platform that securely captures payment information from customers and transmits it to the payment ecosystem for processing.

It acts as a secure bridge between the customer, merchant and payment processor.

Why Do We Need a Gateway?

Security

Protect card and payment data.

Encryption

Encrypt sensitive information.

Fraud Prevention

Reduce payment fraud risk.

Compliance

Support PCI-DSS requirements.

Step-by-Step Working

  1. Customer clicks Pay Now.
  2. Gateway receives payment details.
  3. Data is encrypted securely.
  4. Gateway sends request to processor.
  5. Processor contacts network and bank.
  6. Authorization response returns.
  7. Gateway displays success or failure.

Gateway vs Processor

Payment Gateway

Collects and secures payment information.

Payment Processor

Routes transactions across payment networks.
